Competitive Intelligence

Weaponizing Incumbent
Vulnerabilities

No head-to-head displacement on mega-contracts. Instead, exploit specific, documented weaknesses.

Deloitte
The Trust Deficit
The RIBridges data breach — 650,000 citizens impacted, $6.3M settlement, Brain Cipher dwelling undetected for months despite hundreds of firewall alerts — has created a structural trust deficit with state CIOs.
Strategy: Position IPS cybersecurity and blockchain-based records as the alternative for states reassessing Deloitte relationships.
Tyler Technologies
The Security Achilles Heel
Tyler's LockBit ransomware attack, exposure of sealed court records, and forced class-action settlement create an opening for IPS's secure modernization approach.
Strategy: Target Tyler-dependent courts and justice agencies with secure, modern cloud alternatives.
Gainwell Technologies
Talent Acquisition Target
$6B debt burden, offshoring backlash, and S&P downgrade have destabilized the workforce. Rather than competing for contracts, recruit their best people.
Strategy: Recruit Medicaid systems architects, state CIO relationships, and delivery leads with 15–20% comp premiums.

Risk Management

Risk Factors &
Mitigation

Talent Competition
~5,000 person national talent pool for govt IT domain experts
Recruit from Gainwell (destabilized); 15–20% comp premiums; Infosys internal training pipeline and global bench for rapid scaling.
Long Procurement Cycles
6–18 months from RFP to award delays revenue recognition
Acquisitions provide immediate revenue; cooperative vehicles (NASPO, Sourcewell) bypass competitive procurement entirely.
Incumbent Advantage
Deloitte (25 states), CGI (46-year track record), Tyler (98% retention)
Target modular components (CMS mandates modularity); subcontract on mega-deals for past performance; exploit documented security failures.
Federal Funding Uncertainty
Medicaid cuts, ARPA sunset, SLCGP authorization expiration
Cuts paradoxically increase IT demand — states must implement complex new eligibility rules. Diversify across multiple funding streams.
Onshore Delivery Cost
70–90% onshore mandate compresses margins vs. commercial model
